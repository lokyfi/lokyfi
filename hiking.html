Hiking Report: Hwangnyeongsan Mountain

Overview

To appreciate the natural beauty of Busan and strengthen our connection with the city, we hiked Hwangnyeongsan (황령산), which stands at 427 meters tall. Although this was an individual hike with a friend (not a classmate), we followed the instructions and captured the required deliverables.

Participants

Two hikers participated: myself and a friend (not enrolled in the same class).

Reflection

Hiking Hwangnyeongsan offered an incredible view of Busan, especially after sunset. Despite the relatively short distance, the elevation and winding trail made for a rewarding experience.
