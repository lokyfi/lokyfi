What is Google Apps Script?

Google Apps Script is a cloud-based scripting platform for light-weight application development in the Google Workspace platform. It uses JavaScript and allows users to automate tasks across Google products like Sheets, Docs, Drive, and more.

5Ws of Apps Script

  • Who: Anyone with a Google account can use it.
  • What: It automates repetitive tasks and extends the functionality of Google apps.
  • Where: Primarily used within Google Workspace apps like Sheets and Docs.
  • When: Useful when you need automation, integration, or custom workflows.
  • Why: Saves time and boosts productivity without third-party tools.

Creative Uses of Apps Script

  • Auto-sending personalized emails from Sheets
  • Creating custom spreadsheet functions
  • Fetching data from external APIs into Sheets
  • Automated reports generation
  • Workflow automation for Google Forms responses

Pros and Cons of Apps Script

Pros:

  • Beginner-friendly
  • Works seamlessly with Google apps
  • Completely free and cloud-based

Cons:

  • Limited execution time
  • Dependent on internet connection
  • Restricted to Google ecosystem
